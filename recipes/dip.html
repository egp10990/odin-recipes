Chuy's Jalapeno Ranch Dip

Description

This recipe is a copycat of Chuy's famous Jalapeno Ranch dip. The popular Tex-Mex chain boasts several location across the United States. I have been making this dip for parties and various get-togethers, to the extreme delight of friends and family. I often get asked for the recipe, so thought I would just make a page outlining the ingredients.

The recipe is extremely simple, and comes together in mere minutes. There are only a handful of ingredients, and once made this dip goes well with absolutely anything. Just use your imagination! I've heard friends dip anything from tortilla chips, to Cheez-Its, to various veggies in it.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 paclet dry ranch dip
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 cup cilantro
  • 3/4 cup pickled jalapeno
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tomatillos
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together sour cream, mayonnaise, ranch dip packet, and garlic salt.
  2. In blender, puree cilantro and pickled jalapenos with a little juice. If using tomatillos, blend as well.
  3. And puree to mayo/sour cream mix.
  4. Stir in buttermilk until desired consistency is reached. Add lime juice if using.
  5. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
