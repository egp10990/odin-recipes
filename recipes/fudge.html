Fudge

Description

Nothing beats a good old piece of decadent milk chocolate fudge. This is one of my favorite recipes. You can add optional nuts, or keep it just straight chocolate like I like to do.

This recipe is different in that it uses marshmallow creme. This makes the fudge have a wonderful consistency throughout.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 7-oz jar marshmallow creme
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk chocoalte chips
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Line an 8-inch square pan with aluminum foil.
  2. Combine sugar, marshmallow creme, evaporated milk, butter, and salt in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Bring to full boil and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Remove from heat and add milk chocolate chips and semisweet chocolate chips. Stir until melted and smooth.
  5. Stir in vanilla, then pour in prepared pan. Chill for two hours.
