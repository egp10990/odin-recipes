Grilled Cheese

Description

Who doesn't love grilled cheese? The two ingredients (bread and cheese) are two of the most delicious things ever. Of course, a little butter never hurt either.

I enjoy fancy grilled cheese as much as the next person, but am also a big fan of the classic white bread with American cheese. This recipe is easy as can be.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white or what bread
  • 1 slice of American cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Melt half the tablespoon of butter in a medium pan over medium heat.
  2. Add one slice of bread and top with cheese slice and other bread slice.
  3. Once bread has been toasted to you liking, add the other half of the butter, and flip sandwich to toast the other side.
  4. Serve sandwich whole or cut in half. Tomato soup optional!
